## 1. The decision

The portfolio will keep Ahmed's primary professional identity:

> **Business Growth & Operations Specialist**

It will make his strongest practical depth immediately clear:

> **Customer Experience · Support Operations · Process Improvement · Team Development · Service Quality**

It will retain wider business judgment through a smaller group of clearly bounded supporting lenses:

> **Customer Success Enablement · Customer Journey & Conversion Review · Business Performance Interpretation · Product Requirements & Prioritization · AI-Aware Workflow Decisions · Digital Commerce**

This is a **repositioning of emphasis**, not a career change and not a reduction in seniority.

Ahmed is presented as the person who:

1. understands the business problem;
2. collects and interprets customer, team, process, and performance evidence;
3. traces the likely root cause across the system;
4. defines the outcome, workflow, requirements, and priorities;
5. coordinates the relevant specialists and stakeholders;
6. reviews whether the change produced a better business result.

The portfolio must not imply that Ahmed personally performs every technical, analytical, design, or engineering discipline involved in an implementation.

## 9. Working method

Use:

> **Understand → Diagnose → Prioritize → Define → Coordinate → Measure**

### Understand

Clarify the business context, customer, current process, goal, constraints, and available evidence.

### Diagnose

Separate the visible symptom from the likely root cause; map cross-functional seams and missing evidence.

### Prioritize

Rank problems and opportunities by business impact, recurrence, confidence, risk, effort, and feasibility.

### Define

Define the future-state outcome, workflow, responsibilities, requirements, success criteria, and what should not be built.

### Coordinate

Translate the decision into clear briefs and align the relevant business, service, content, design, product, or technical specialists.

### Measure

Specify the baseline, KPI, review period, and evidence needed to judge whether the change worked.

The method must not imply that Ahmed personally performs every implementation discipline.

## 13. Public proof and evidence

The evidence system remains unchanged:

- verified facts;
- calculated findings;
- user-asserted work;
- illustrative scenarios;
- independent public observations;
- confidential work.

### Additional rule

The portfolio must distinguish **professional breadth** from **technical craft ownership**.

A claim may be true at the business-decision level while still requiring attribution for design, engineering, analytics, or implementation. The Scope of Contribution section is mandatory for that reason.

### Alpha Capital status

The following are currently user-asserted, not verified:

- progression from Customer Support Specialist to Customer Manager after two years;
- contribution to Arabic-market communication, customer experience, service quality, and operational improvement;
- coaching / knowledge-sharing contribution;
- awards and review totals until documents are supplied.

They may be drafted as attributed user-asserted experience but may not receive a Verified Evidence badge or prominent metric treatment without proof.

### Evidence and interview-defensibility gate

Every public capability must pass all five tests before it is published:

1. **Real example:** Ahmed can name a real project, situation, or recurring pattern.
2. **Scope:** Ahmed can explain what he owned, defined, recommended, coordinated, interpreted, and did not own.
3. **Evidence:** There is a document, screen, public source, approved statement, or clearly labelled user-confirmed account.
4. **Method:** Ahmed can explain how he reached the conclusion, not only repeat the conclusion.
5. **Interview answer:** Ahmed can explain the capability clearly in two minutes without relying on technical language he does not understand.

If a capability fails any test, it must be:

- removed from the public page;
- moved from a core capability to a supporting lens;
- labelled as an illustrative scenario or independent analysis;
- or rewritten as a narrower, defensible contribution.

Approved public evidence labels:

- Verified Evidence
- User-Confirmed Real Work
- Founder-Led Work
- Anonymized Real Work
- Illustrative Scenario
- Independent Analysis
- Client-Confidential

These public labels do not replace the more detailed internal claim statuses used by the evidence register.
